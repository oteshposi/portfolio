About Me

Driven by curiosity, innovation, and a deep passion for problem-solving, I am OLAMIPOSI OTESILE, a future-focused Mechatronics enthusiast, aspiring engineer, and dedicated researcher. With a strong foundation in physics and engineering, my journey has been shaped by a relentless desire to explore the intersection of science, technology, and human impact.

I founded Euretix, a start-up rooted in the idea that solutions to overlooked problems can transform the world. Under the motto “Find a way, or make one,” Euretix seeks to merge scientific research with engineering to create innovative solutions that address both everyday challenges and more complex global issues.

Beyond my entrepreneurial spirit, I have a solid academic and hands-on background in full-stack development, graphic design, programming, and more. I’ve had the privilege of interning and volunteering at various organizations, where I applied all the skills I've learned over the years to develop solutions and provide help.

My research focuses on wave and particle dynamics, delving into the physics of air, sound, and light, and seeking real-world applications for complex scientific principles. Recently, I have worked and published various physics and engineering research papers, one of them titled “The Hypothetical Implications of Creating a Perfect Vacuum on the Fabric of the Universe,” exploring the potential consequences of achieving a perfect vacuum in space-time.

I believe that the blend of mechatronics and physics will equip me with the knowledge and tools to create transformative technologies.

As a lifelong learner, my interests continue to expand. I am currently exploring AI, app development, and machine learning. I believe the STEM field can and should be used to solve global problems

With a mindset anchored in discovery, problem-solving, and impact, I am committed to driving forward the next wave of technological advancements. Welcome to my portfolio, and I invite you to explore my journey of innovation and the projects I’m passionate about.
